  • April 2024 - May 31, 2024

    Pilgrimage

    1100KM PILGRIMAGE ON THE CAMINO DE SANTIAGO (FRENCH WAY)

    Started from Saint-Jean-Pied-de-Port in France, climbing over the Pyrenees into Spain.

    Traveled east to west across Spain, passing through Pamplona, Burgos, León, and reaching Santiago de Compostela.

    Continued westward to the coastal towns of Fisterra (The End of the World) and Muxia (Rebirth of Life).

    Headed south to Porto in Portugal, completing the pilgrimage.

  • November 2023

    Trekking

    SUMMITING MOUNT KILIMANJARO IN TANZANIA

    Followed the Machame Route from the foothills and trekked/climbed Mount Kilimanjaro for a total distance of 62 km.

    Successfully reached the summit of Kilimanjaro(5,895m) and returned in 7 days with the assistance of local guides.

  • June 2023 - September 2023

    Electric Vehicle

    ELECTRIC VEHICLE ROAD TRIP AROUND CHINA & G318 TIBET ROUTE

    Traveled from Shanghai northward along the coast, passing through Lianyungang, Qingdao, Zibo, Tianjin, and Chengde, reaching Beijing.

    Headed west through Datong, Shijiazhuang, Taiyuan, Xi’an, Hanzhong, and Guangyuan, reaching Chengdu.

    Followed the G318 Sichuan-Tibet Route from Chengdu to Lhasa.

    Continued from Lhasa through Shigatze to the Everest Base Camp (5,300m).

    Returned to Lhasa and traveled south along the G214 Yunnan-Tibet Route to Kunming.

    Traveled eastward through Baise, Guilin, Liuzhou, Changsha, and Nanchang, and finally returning to Shanghai.
